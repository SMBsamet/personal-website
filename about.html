SAMET BOZKURT

More than just a developer—a problem solver, a lifelong learner, and a tech enthusiast.

My Story

My journey into the world of technology began not in a classroom, but with a spark of curiosity. I was fascinated by how a few lines of text could create entire worlds and solve real-world problems. This fascination quickly turned into a passion.

I wrote my first "Hello, World!" program, and from that moment, I was hooked. The thrill of building something from scratch, debugging complex issues, and seeing my code come to life is what drives me every day. Choosing to study Computer Engineering at Muğla Sıtkı Koçman University was a natural next step to turn this passion into a profession.

Now, I'm channeling that energy into mastering web technologies, exploring software architecture, and contributing to projects that make a difference. Every challenge is a new opportunity to learn and grow.

My Development Philosophy

Clean & Efficient Code

I believe that readable, maintainable, and efficient code is the foundation of any great software. It's not just about making it work; it's about making it right.

User-Centric Design

Technology should serve people. I focus on creating intuitive and accessible user experiences that solve real problems for the end-user.

Continuous Learning

The tech world is always evolving, and so am I. I am committed to being a lifelong learner, constantly adapting to new tools and paradigms.
